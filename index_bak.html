ABOUT

Infinity specialises in projects that are owner operated or owned as long term assets. Our objective is to focus on safely delievering within program and budget and importantly, strive to deliver an end product that is constructed to a standard that will ensure minimum long term maintenance.
History

Infinity was established in May 1992. Now 22 years on, the company remains privately owned and has eight (8) active partner / directors, who bring a wealth of design, construction, project management and engineering experience including water treatment and ground water contamination and remediation expertise.

The company has successfully delivered over 400 diverse projects, for a broad range of industry sectors and often competes in the Tier 2 and Tier 3 Contractor segment.

Since the establishment of Infinity, we have always been dedicated to providing professional project management and construction services of excellence.

Resources

Infinity is a Proprietary Limited Company, with eight shareholders each of whom are actively involved in the operation on a daily basis. Currently we employ 54 staff and have an annual turnover in excess of $55 million.

We have a strong financial position which is underpinned by sensible business practices. Infinity has no debt and, moreover, we strive to maintain a minimum of two years profit in retained earnings. Our financial reports are available upon request.

The success of Infinity is largely due to the proactive daily involvement of all of the directors who are also shareholders. Our staff display a diverse range of skill sets from disciplines such as civil engineering, quantity surveying, construction management and environmental science.

Consultants

Whether we are managing a design team or working under instruction, we aim to always work in a collaborative manner to ensure all design decisions are well researched, costed and are appropriate for the intended use.

Suppliers & Subcontractors

Infinity has a excellent reputation for payment with suppliers and subcontractors, this acknowledgment results in timely delivery, exceptional quality, workmanship, materials, and service.

Management Systems

In 2011 we achieved certification to Quality Management System ISO 9001:2008, Occupational Health and Management System AS/NZS 4801:2001 and Environmental Management System ISO 14001:2004.

Management Structure

Infinity has eight directors all of whom play an active role in the day to day running of the business. Currently, we have eight project managers, three estimators, a chartered quantity surveyor 12, Site Managers, and a pool of leading hands and labourers. In addition, we have an office based administration and finance division totalling five staff.

22 Years of Excellence.

For 21 Years, Infinity has maintained the prime objective of delivering true quality and great service. We have been privileged enough to be involved with many iconic projects with great clients and experienced professional project teams. We believe in building to exacting standards every time. We take pride in every project, irrespective of budget, nature or scope. We are able to focus on “the detail”, as a result of correct allocation of resources, right from the onset. Whether cost planning, programming or constructing, we focus on meeting client objectives.

TEAM


The founder of the company Alan Yazbek holds the Managing Director’s position and is supported by the balance of directors, each of which play a key role in each division of the company.

Infinity commits key personnel to a project and these members are usually active from the design or tender phase, right through the construction phase and up until the finalisation of the defects period.

Our policy is to select key project personnel with the ability to best undertake the scope of work and work collaboratively with all team members. Once committed our staff remain committed for the duration of the project.
